Our Story

Coffee Roasted with Intention

Ember & Oak was born from a simple obsession — the perfect cup. We source single-origin beans from ethical farms across Ethiopia, Colombia, and Guatemala, roasting each batch in-house to unlock every note of flavor.

Our café is designed to be your third place — not home, not work, but somewhere in between. A place to slow down, breathe, and savour.
